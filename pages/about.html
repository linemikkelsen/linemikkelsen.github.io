About
I am a linguist working with language communities in California, Denmark and Greenland. This work combines theoretical and analytic work on syntax with work organized around language maintenance and language revitalization.

I am a core faculty member in the Designated Emphasis in Indigenous Language Revitalization.

I am affiliated with the Department of Philosophy.
